What Is A Test Drive?

A test drive is an opportunity to get a much clearer idea of what a car is like that will help you make your purchase decision. The test drive puts you in the driver's seat, allowing you to find out everything you need to know or to simply make sure you're comfortable with the car's driving experience and technology.

Can I Test Drive a car at First Choice Cars?

At First Choice Cars, we try to make the test drive experience as simple as possible.

When you arrive for your test drive, you can expect the following:

· One of our staff members will bring the used car you are interested in buying

· You will move to the driver's seat, and the staff will present you with the keys

· You will have all the time you need to get accustomed to the steering controls and layout of the interior

· All the seats can be adjusted along with the rearview and wing mirrors to ensure you feel safe and comfortable before driving off.

This early stage of the test drive is usually a great point to learn more about the specific features of the vehicle, including on-board technology and safety features, and we can even demonstrate how to set up and use the different features of the car as needed.

BENEFITS OF BOOKING A TEST DRIVE

At First Choice Cars, we strongly encourage our customers to take a test drive before purchasing a vehicle. The act of test driving offers numerous advantages and is particularly important when considering a pre-owned car. Given that a car is often the second largest financial investment a person will make, it's important to have as much information as possible to make an informed decision and feel confident in your choice.

Some of the benefits include:

· Allows you to gain firsthand experience of what it's like to drive the vehicle and see if it aligns with your driving preferences

· Gives you time to familiarize yourself with the car's handling and performance

· The opportunity to assess the comfort and support of the car, such as the driving position and seat adjustability, in a real-world setting

· Allows you to assess the car's accessibility and storage features with your family

· If you have young children, you can bring along child seats or strollers to ensure they fit properly (our staff can also provide information on safety features such as ISO fixings)

Our knowledgeable staff at First Choice Cars will be on hand to answer any questions and demonstrate how the car's features work, allowing you to make an informed decision about the vehicle's suitability for your needs.
